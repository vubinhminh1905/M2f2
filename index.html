One Rose is enough for the Dawn

How cunningly nature hides every wrinkle of her inconceivable antiquity under Roses and Violets and morning dew!

ON THE OUTSIDE, YOU’RE DOING FINE.

Perhaps better than fine; perhaps life looks like you always hoped it would.

But inside, the picture is different. Daily life leaves you feeling overwhelmed and stressed. Your achievements don’t feel like successes. Past experiences make you feel stuck and anxious. You feel unable to be your authentic self, to connect with the people and things that matter most.

By supporting you to better understand yourself, using evidence based clinical psychology approaches, I will help you shift from feeling burnt out to feeling balanced.

So that you wake each day with more gratitude, positivity and purpose. So that you live each day with more self-compassion, meaning and mindfulness. So that you face hard days with more resilience, calm and control.

I will help you stop surviving and start thriving. So that you don’t just live, but you live well.
